We Find Resources and Events

We find online resources and events for the NYC area that will help you contribute and support social justice movements that matter to you, while keeping you and those around you safe during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Non-Profits Reachout

Non-profit organizations can contact us to share information on resources and events in the NYC area that they need help promoting. This includes:

    • Petitions
    • Fundraising
    • Donation Collection
    • Contacting Local Leaders
    • Voting

We Inform You As You Catch Up On The News

When you browse the news on the New York Times or the Washington Post on your desktop computer, our Google Chrome extension will auotmatically replace images on the page with images that will inform you about the resources and events we have gathered for you within the NYC area.

For more information about these resources and events, simply click the image and you will be redirected to the appropriate website.

While these images do auto-load on the home page of these news sites, they can be turned off to reveal the original news source images, or you can escape politics with 'cuddle mode'.
